Andy Murray, Serena Williams advance at Miami

World No. 1 Serena Williams reached the semi-finals and British second seed Andy Murray booked a quarter-final berth with straight-set triumphs on Tuesday at the WTA and ATP Miami Masters.
Williams defeated Chinese fifth seed Li Na 6-3, 7-6 (7/5) while Murray downed Italian 16th seed Andreas Seppi 6-2, 6-4 at the $8.5 million hardcourt tournament.
